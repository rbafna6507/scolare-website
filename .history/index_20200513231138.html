Tutoro

Tutoro is a soon-to-be 501(c)(3) non-profit that provides one on one tutoring to under-resourced or struggling students locally and nationwide. We enable students to excel in school and reach their goals to ultimately empower other underserved communities.

  • Learn more

What we do

We connect tutors that excel in various subjects to underserved and struggling students locally and nationwide to enable them to reach their goals and excel in school.

1. Partner

Tutoro partners with various nearby and far away medical centers to collect surplus medical supplies that would otherwise be unused or thrown away.

2. Verify

Tutoro works with partnered medical centers to verify medical supply quality and integrity. Supplies are then sorted and prepared for collection.

3. Collect

These supplies are transported to a secure storage facility, where they are then stored until a sufficient quantity for donation has been collected.

4. Donate

Tutoro donates to overseas and domestic medical centers and communities that need these vital medical supplies to save lives.
