Remedi

Remedi is a soon-to-be 501(c)(3) non-profit medical surplus recovery organization (MSRO) that provides sustainable solutions to inefficient health care resource distribution. We reallocate excess, unused medical supplies—that would otherwise be discarded—from U.S. hospitals and suppliers to areas of need overseas or domestic. We redistrubute vital medical equipment to hospitals & medical offices in need.
